Secret Art

Makes you to transfer messages that can be destroyed anytime but cannot be replicated ;)

INTRODUCTION


I started doing different experiments with gelatine so that I get to know it as a material. I froze it, ironed it, UV printed it then ground it and mixed it with other substances to see how it reacts. I wanted to push the limits gelatine has because it is highly connected with the food industry and the colourful images related to candies that we have in our minds.

I want to think of it as a new tool for publishing your work temporarily that can be stored and destroyed anytime. I want it to be about graphic designers who want to have a material that they can do at home with own convenience and then destroy. It is interesting to be able to produce something on your own and apply it to your practice. So, I decided to focus on how this creation and destruction works. I want to make gelatine as a new potential for a new material “that can be destroyed anytime without anyone knowing it”. The destruction of the artwork on this special paper can be interesting if someone wants to convey a message for a short point of time or create a temporary publication. This material is very delicate so, the size of it is relatively small that is due to the fact that the material will crumble so, it can be only be made on a small scale. The benefits of using the small format of this material more efficient to transmit secret meanings or messages via post, if you are brave enough. By using heat, the particles of gelatine respond and become water-soluble. In this manner, the artwork is lost and is transformed into water and ultimately nobody knows anymore of its existence and what had been depicted on it.

Description

MAKE ENJOY DESTROY

How to give away a dangerous secret or host your temporary exhibition with the new material made out of cornstarch, gelatine and water and printing on the material with any kind of method you want. The creation of this new material is complimented by the destruction of the material.

Instructions

YOU WILL NEED

You will need: 1 pack of gelatine leafs and 1 pack of cornstarch and a blender

GRIND

Break the gelatine leafs into smaller pieces to fit in the blender. Measure 100ml of cornstarch and 50ml of water. Hit start until all powdery. Empty the mixture on a flat surface and smoothen the surface by using baking paper or something flat, let it dry.

CREATE YOUR MASTERPIECE OR MESSAGE ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)

You can use any machine of your prefference depending on the design you will make. You can choose from UV flatbed to screen print to hand paint with acrylic or just normal pencil. Be careful, it doesn't go into the normal inkjet printers!

SHARE

Now that your design is done, you can tell your secret to the person you want to. If you are brave enough you can put it in an envelope and send it via post.

DESTROY

In order to destroy the material you will need to submerge it into boiling hot water. The time depends on the intensity of the stove.
